# Spam Word Blacklist

Aligned with Mailmeteor and major ESPs. Avoid these in subject lines AND body copy.

---

## Hard Avoid (Will Trigger Filters)

### Urgency Words
```
act now
limited time
hurry
urgent
immediately
instant
now
don't delay
expire
last chance
deadline
only [X] left
ending soon
```

### Money Words
```
free
$
$$
$$$
cash
money
save
discount
cheap
lowest price
bargain
bonus
prize
winner
credit
no cost
no fee
double your
earn extra
extra income
```

### Overpromise Words
```
guaranteed
100%
promise
amazing
incredible
unbelievable
miracle
revolutionary
breakthrough
secret
exclusive
once in a lifetime
best ever
```

### Spam Classics
```
click here
click below
click now
buy now
order now
call now
apply now
sign up free
subscribe
unsubscribe
opt-in
opt-out
dear friend
dear [generic]
congratulations
you've been selected
you've won
as seen on
```

### Formatting Triggers
```
ALL CAPS WORDS
Multiple exclamation marks!!!
% symbol (always spell out "percent")
Excessive punctuation...???
RE: or FW: (fake threading)
[brackets] in subject lines (unless spintax)
```

---

## Soft Avoid (Context-Dependent)

These can work in the right context but use sparingly:

```
opportunity
solution
help
increase
improve
results
success
growth
roi
performance
optimize
transform
```

**Rule:** One soft-avoid word per email max. Never in subject line.

---

## Safe Alternatives

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| free | complimentary, no charge, included |
| guarantee | track record, proven, consistent |
| amazing | solid, strong, notable |
| click here | [just use descriptive link text] |
| buy now | worth a conversation? |
| limited time | [remove urgency entirely] |
| % | percent (spelled out) or rephrase |
| save money | reduce costs, cut spend |
| increase revenue | grow, improve, lift |

---

## Pre-Send Checklist

Before any email goes out:

```
[ ] No % symbol anywhere
[ ] No $ or money symbols
[ ] No urgency words
[ ] No overpromise words
[ ] No ALL CAPS (except acronyms like CIO)
[ ] No multiple exclamation marks
[ ] No "click here" or "buy now"
[ ] No fake RE:/FW: threading
[ ] Subject line clean
[ ] P.S. line clean
```

---

## Why This Matters

Modern spam filters use machine learning trained on billions of emails. They don't just look for individual wordsâ€”they look for patterns.

An email with:
- "Free" in the subject
- "$" in the body
- "Act now" as a CTA
- "!!!" anywhere

...will almost certainly hit spam, even if each element alone might pass.

**The goal:** Sound like a human having a professional conversation, not a marketer trying to sell something.
