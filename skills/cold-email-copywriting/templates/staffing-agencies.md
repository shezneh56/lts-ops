# Staffing Agency Email Templates

Use these for recruiting firms, staffing agencies, and talent acquisition companies.

---

## Personas

| Title | Pain Points | Lead Angles |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| CEO/President | Growth, competitive differentiation | V5 (Credibility), V7 (Direct Value) |
| VP Operations | Efficiency, placement velocity | V1 (Journey), V6 (Problem) |

```
P.S. That {client|organization} {processes|handles|manages} {thousands of|10K+} applications {monthly|annually} - their site {finally keeps pace|now supports that volume|actually converts}.
```

## Vertical Specializations

Customize for staffing verticals:

**Healthcare Staffing:**
- credential verification, license compliance
- shift-based scheduling
- per diem, travel nursing

**IT/Tech Staffing:**
- skills matching, tech stack filtering
- contract vs. permanent
- remote/hybrid preferences

**Light Industrial:**
- high volume, fast apply
- location-based search
- shift scheduling

**Executive Search:**
- confidential applications
- credential-heavy profiles
- relationship-driven

**Finance/Accounting:**
- credential verification (CPA, CFA)
- compliance requirements
- contract and perm mix
