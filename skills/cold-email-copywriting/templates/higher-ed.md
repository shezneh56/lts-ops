# Higher Education Email Templates

Use these as starting points for higher ed campaigns. Customize proof points and spintax for each prospect.

---

## Personas

| Title | Pain Points | Lead Angles |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Vice Chancellor | Strategic vision, enrollment metrics, student outcomes | V5 (Credibility), V7 (Direct Value) |
| VP Digital/Marketing | Website performance, enrollment conversions | V1 (Journey), V6 (Problem) |
| CIO | Platform decisions, integrations, accessibility | V3 (Accessibility), V2 (Navigation) |
| Director of Web/Digital | Daily UX, site management, search | V2 (Navigation), V6 (Problem) |
| Dean of Students | Student-facing processes, self-service | V1 (Journey), V4 (Custom) |

## Variation 1: Enrollment Journey

**Subject:** `{student enrollment|enrollment experience|the enrollment journey} at {COMPANY}`

**Body:**
```

We {redesigned|rebuilt|transformed} the enrollment journey for a {79,000-student college district|major community college system|large multi-campus district} and watched {completion rates climb|enrollment improve|conversion rates rise}.

{SENDER_EMAIL_SIGNATURE}

P.S. That district {awarded|granted|conferred} over 10,000 degrees last year - their site {finally keeps pace with demand|now matches their scale|actually supports that volume}.
```
