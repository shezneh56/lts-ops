# B2B SaaS Email Templates

Use these for enterprise software, SaaS platforms, and tech company campaigns.

---

## Personas

| Title | Pain Points | Lead Angles |
|-------|-------------|-------------|

## B2B-Specific Adaptations

When adapting proof points for B2B SaaS:

| Higher Ed Stat | B2B Adaptation |
|----------------|----------------|
| 79,000 students | "enterprise-scale users" or "79K+ users" |
| 350+ programs | "complex product portfolio" or "hundreds of offerings" |
| 10,000+ degrees | "thousands of conversions" |
| enrollment journey | "buyer journey" |
| students | "prospects" or "buyers" |
| programs | "solutions" or "products" |
| campus | "product line" or "business unit" |
