## Campaign Overview
- **Target:** US Community Colleges
- **Angle:** Alamo Colleges case study / best practice sharing
- **Sequence:** 3 emails over 5 days
- **ICP:** CMO, CTO, Digital Experience leads, Marketing Directors at community colleges

## Key Messaging Points (FROM ACTUAL CASE STUDY)
- **10+ year partnership** with Alamo Colleges (trust/longevity)
- **2023 DXP upgrade** with Optimizely (modernization)
- **Search and Navigation improvements** (findability)
- **Streamlined enrollment journey** (conversion focus)
- **Student-centric digital experience** (mission alignment)
- C2: 2024 Optimizely Partner of the Year

## Case Study Results (Verified)
- Site more navigable
- Search results more relevant
- Content more accessible
- Better serving diverse student base
- Streamlined journey for future enrollees

**Body:**
```
{Hi|Hey|Hello} {{first_name}},

{Thought you might find this interesting|This might be relevant for you|Figured this could be useful} — we've been partnering with Alamo Colleges for over a decade on their digital ecosystem. The work has helped them better serve their diverse student base while staying true to their student-centric mission.

{Would it be helpful if I shared|Want me to send over|Should I share} the case study? {Happy to send it your way|No strings attached|Just thought it might spark some ideas for {{college_name}}}.

{Best|Cheers|Talk soon},
{{sender_name}}
```

## Spam Avoidance Checklist

✅ No percentage symbols  
✅ No made-up statistics  
✅ No urgency words (act now, limited time, etc.)  
✅ No "click here" or "click" language  
✅ No ALL CAPS  
✅ No excessive punctuation  
✅ No financial/money claims  
✅ No "guaranteed" or overpromise words  
✅ No "free" language  
✅ Conversational, natural tone  
✅ Soft CTAs throughout  
✅ Subject lines are clean and personalized
